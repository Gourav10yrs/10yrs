National Democratic Alliance NDA

National Democratic Alliance (N.D.A.) Previous Years Question paper and Answer Key

NDA (National Defence Academy) exams are conducted twice every year by UPSC i.e. NDA1 & NDA2. The exam is conducted in written mode in which candidates have to fill up the answers in an OMR sheet .Questions are set in two different languages English & Hindi. Candidates can choose the language of examination according to their convenience.

Syllabus for NDA includes topics in mathematics, English and general knowledge. A candidate appearing in N.D.A. & N.A. should definitely be Indian and have qualified 12th or equivalent examination from any recognized board with a good body built and males should not get married till their training period is over. The minimum age should be 16 for appearing in the exam.

After clearing both the papers, shortlisted candidates are sent to Khadakwasla(near Pune) where training takes place for the three cadets the navy, the army & the air force.

At NDA, all cadets of Army, air force and navy are trained together for 3 years together, after which they are given B.SC Degree in science. After which, Army cadets goes to IMA, Air Force cadet goes to AFA Hyderabad and, Naval cadet goes to INA Ezhimala, Kerala.

Life in NDA is all about learning discipline & adhering self-improvement. We wish good luck for the upcoming caretakers of India.
